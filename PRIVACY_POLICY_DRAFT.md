# Privacy Policy Draft

Maths Formula Memorizer Offline is designed to work fully offline.

The app does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data.

The app does not require login.
The app does not use advertising.
The app does not use analytics.
The app does not request internet permission.

Progress, quiz results, weak formulas, and streak information are saved locally on the user's device only. This data is not sent anywhere.

If the app is deleted, local progress may be removed from the device.
