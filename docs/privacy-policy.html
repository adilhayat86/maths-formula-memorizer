Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Maths Formula Memorizer Offline is designed to work fully offline.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data outside the user's device.

  • No login is required.
  • No advertising is used.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No Firebase, server, or external backend is used.
  • No internet permission is requested.

Local Data Stored On The Device

The app may save student name, selected class, progress, quiz results, weak formulas, and streak information locally on the user's device only. This information is not sent anywhere.

Student Name

The app may ask for a student name so it can show friendly coaching messages inside the app. The name is saved only on the user's device. It is not sent to the developer, uploaded to a server, or shared with anyone.

Deleting Data

Users can reset progress inside the app. If the app is deleted from the device, local app data may also be removed from the device.
